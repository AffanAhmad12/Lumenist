LUMELIGHT — MATERNITY BENEFIT POLICY
Document Owner: Human Resources
Effective Date: 1 April 2026
Last Reviewed: 1 April 2026
Next Review Due: 31 March 2027
Document Version: 1.0
Applicable To: All confirmed and probationary women employees, in line with the Maternity
Benefit Act, 1961 (as amended)

1. PURPOSE
This policy outlines the maternity-related benefits available to women employees at Lumelight,
in compliance with the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961 (as amended in 2017), and Lumelight's own
supportive provisions for new parents.

2. ELIGIBILITY
- Applicable to all women employees who have worked at Lumelight for at least 80 days in the
  12 months immediately preceding the expected date of delivery
- Applicable regardless of employment grade or department (HR, Finance, BPO, LPO, IT,
  Training, etc.)

3. DURATION OF MATERNITY LEAVE
- 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the first and second child
- 12 weeks of paid maternity leave for the third child onward
- Of the 26-week entitlement, up to 8 weeks may be availed before the expected delivery date,
  with the remainder following after delivery
- 12 weeks of paid maternity leave for a woman who legally adopts a child below the age of 3
  months, or for a commissioning mother in surrogacy, from the date the child is handed over

4. MISCARRIAGE AND MEDICAL TERMINATION
In the case of a miscarriage or medical termination of pregnancy, the employee is entitled to 6
weeks of paid leave immediately following the date of the event, on production of a medical
certificate.

5. PAY DURING MATERNITY LEAVE
- Full average salary (basic + applicable allowances, as defined under the Act) is paid for the
  entire duration of maternity leave
- Payment is processed through the regular payroll cycle; no separate claim process is required
  beyond submitting medical documentation to HR

6. MEDICAL BONUS
A medical bonus, as prescribed under the Maternity Benefit Act, is paid where prenatal
confinement and postnatal care are not provided free of cost by Lumelight, applicable to
employees not covered under the company's group medical insurance for maternity-related
hospitalization.

7. NURSING BREAKS
Upon return from maternity leave, the employee is entitled to two nursing breaks of prescribed
duration each day, until the child completes 15 months of age, in addition to standard rest
intervals.

8. CRECHE FACILITY
Where applicable as per location and headcount thresholds under law, Lumelight provides or
facilitates access to a creche facility. Employees may visit the creche up to 4 times a day,
including rest intervals.

9. WORK FROM HOME OPTION
Subject to the nature of the role and mutual agreement between the employee and manager,
Lumelight may permit work-from-home arrangements after maternity leave, for a mutually
agreed period, where job responsibilities reasonably allow for remote work.

10. INTIMATION REQUIREMENTS
- Employees should inform their manager and HR in writing at least 8 weeks before the
  expected date of delivery, along with a doctor's certificate confirming the expected delivery
  date
- A confirmation of actual delivery date should be submitted to HR within 7 days of childbirth for
  payroll and benefit processing

11. JOB PROTECTION
- An employee's position, grade, and salary are protected during maternity leave
- Lumelight does not terminate or issue notice of termination to an employee during her
  maternity leave, except for proven gross misconduct following due process under the
  Disciplinary Policy
- On return, the employee is reinstated to the same position or an equivalent role with no less
  favorable terms

12. ADOPTION AND SURROGACY
Adoptive mothers (adopting a child below 3 months) and commissioning mothers in surrogacy
arrangements are entitled to 12 weeks of paid leave from the date the child is handed over to
them, as outlined in Section 3.

13. PATERNITY LEAVE CROSS-REFERENCE
Paternity leave for the spouse/partner of the employee, or for male employees becoming
fathers, is covered separately under the Leave Policy (10 working days).

14. RETURN TO WORK SUPPORT
- HR conducts a returning-parent check-in within the first 2 weeks of the employee's return to
  discuss any support needed (flexible hours, workload ramp-up, etc.)
- Lumelight encourages managers to facilitate a smooth transition back to full responsibilities
  over a reasonable period

15. NON-DISCRIMINATION
No woman employee shall be discriminated against in recruitment, promotion, or any other
employment decision on grounds of pregnancy or maternity leave availed.

16. CONTACT
For maternity benefit queries, contact HR Helpdesk: hr.helpdesk@lumelight.com (demo address)
