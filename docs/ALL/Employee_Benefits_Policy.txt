LUMELIGHT — EMPLOYEE BENEFITS POLICY
Document Owner: Human Resources
Effective Date: 1 April 2026
Last Reviewed: 1 April 2026
Next Review Due: 31 March 2027
Document Version: 1.0
Applicable To: All confirmed employees (some benefits vary for probationary employees, as noted)

1. PURPOSE
This document summarizes the benefits available to Lumelight employees in addition to base
salary, covering health, financial, and well-being benefits.

2. HEALTH INSURANCE
- Group Mediclaim Policy covering the employee, spouse, and up to 2 dependent children
- Sum insured: INR 3,00,000 per family floater (standard grade); higher grades may have
  enhanced cover as per grade matrix
- Coverage begins from the date of joining for confirmed employees; probationary employees are
  covered from day one as well under Lumelight's policy
- Pre-existing diseases are covered as per insurer terms (no waiting period under the current
  group policy)
- Cashless treatment available at network hospitals; reimbursement claims for non-network
  hospitals processed within 15 working days of submission

3. PERSONAL ACCIDENT AND LIFE INSURANCE
- Group Personal Accident cover: INR 5,00,000 per employee
- Group Term Life Insurance cover: 2x annual fixed salary, subject to insurer terms
- Nomination details must be updated by the employee via the HR portal at the time of joining
  and whenever personal circumstances change

4. PROVIDENT FUND (PF)
- Statutory Provident Fund contribution as per the Employees' Provident Fund Act
- Employee contributes 12% of basic salary; Lumelight matches with an equal contribution
- PF account (UAN) details are shared during onboarding; employees can track contributions via
  the EPFO portal

5. GRATUITY
As per the Payment of Gratuity Act, employees who complete 5 years of continuous service are
eligible for gratuity, calculated as per statutory formula, payable at the time of separation.

6. LEAVE ENCASHMENT
- Unused Privilege/Earned Leave above the carry-forward limit (30 days) may be encashed once
  a year, in the month of March, at the basic salary rate
- Full encashment of accumulated Privilege Leave is processed at the time of final separation

7. WELLNESS AND EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE
- Access to a confidential Employee Assistance Program (EAP) offering counseling support for
  stress, mental health, and personal issues
- Annual health check-up camp organized at major office locations
- Subsidized gym/wellness membership reimbursement up to INR 5,000 per year (where
  applicable per grade)

8. MEALS AND TRANSPORT
- Subsidized cafeteria facility at major office locations
- Shift allowance and pick-up/drop facility for employees working night shifts (BPO/voice
  process), as per the transport policy for the specific location
- Conveyance/transport allowance as per grade and location, included in the salary structure

9. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT BENEFITS
- Reimbursement for job-related certifications, subject to manager and HR approval, up to a
  defined annual limit per grade
- Access to internal learning platform for upskilling in process, communication, and leadership
  skills
- Tuition assistance for relevant higher education programs, subject to a separate policy and
  minimum service commitment post-completion

10. REFERRAL BONUS
Employees referring a successful candidate (who completes the probation period) are eligible
for a referral bonus, with amounts varying by role criticality, as published periodically by Talent
Acquisition.

11. RECOGNITION AND REWARDS
- Monthly and quarterly recognition programs for top performers across BPO, LPO, and support
  functions
- Service milestone awards at 1, 3, 5, 10, and 15 years of continuous service

12. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY BENEFITS
Covered in detail under the separate Maternity Benefit Policy document and the Leave Policy
(for Paternity Leave).

13. RETIREMENT AND SEPARATION BENEFITS
On separation, eligible employees receive full and final settlement including unpaid salary, leave
encashment, gratuity (if eligible), and any other statutory dues, processed within 45 days of the
last working day.

14. CHANGES TO BENEFITS
Lumelight reviews its benefits structure annually and reserves the right to modify, add, or
discontinue benefits with reasonable advance notice to employees, in compliance with
applicable law.

15. CONTACT
For benefit-related queries, contact HR Helpdesk: hr.helpdesk@lumelight.com (demo address)
