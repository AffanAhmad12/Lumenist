1. PURPOSE
This policy describes the types of leave available to Lumelight employees, eligibility, accrual,
and the process for applying for and approving leave.

2. LEAVE YEAR
The Lumelight leave year runs from 1 April to 31 March. Leave balances are reset and
recalculated at the start of each leave year, subject to the carry-forward rules below.

3. TYPES OF LEAVE AND ENTITLEMENT

3.1 Casual Leave (CL)
- 12 days per year, credited at 1 day per completed month of service
- Used for short personal matters; minimum half-day, maximum 3 consecutive days without
  manager approval for longer durations
- Cannot be combined with Privilege Leave beyond 3 consecutive days without prior approval

3.2 Sick Leave (SL)
- 12 days per year, credited at 1 day per completed month of service
- A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days
- Unused sick leave up to 6 days may be carried forward to the next leave year

3.3 Privilege/Earned Leave (PL/EL)
- 15 days per year, accrued monthly
- Must generally be applied for at least 7 days in advance
- Up to 30 days may be accumulated and carried forward; encashment rules are covered in the
  Employee Benefits document

3.4 Maternity Leave
- Governed by the separate Maternity Benefit Policy document

3.5 Paternity Leave
- 10 working days, to be availed within 3 months of the child's birth or adoption

3.6 Bereavement Leave
- 5 working days in the event of the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child,
  parent, sibling)

3.7 Marriage Leave
- 3 working days, available once during employment with Lumelight, on the employee's own
  wedding

3.8 Leave Without Pay (LWP)
- May be granted at management discretion when an employee has exhausted other leave
  balances and has a genuine need
- Extended LWP beyond 30 days requires Head of HR approval and may affect benefit accrual

4. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
Lumelight observes a list of public holidays published annually on the HR intranet, typically
including 10-12 holidays depending on work location, in compliance with applicable state
holiday lists.

5. WEEKLY OFFS
Standard weekly off is 2 days, typically Saturday and Sunday for non-voice teams. BPO/voice
process teams follow a rotational weekly off schedule aligned with shift rosters, communicated
by team leads at least one week in advance.

6. HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE
- All leave must be applied for through the HR portal at least 24 hours in advance (except sick
  leave, which may be applied for retroactively with manager notification on the day of absence)
- Leave is approved by the direct reporting manager; HR is notified automatically through the
  system
- Emergency leave should be communicated to the manager via call/message as early as possible,
  with formal application filed within 2 working days of returning to work

7. UNAPPROVED ABSENCE
Absence without approved leave or notification to the manager for 3 or more consecutive days
may be treated as unauthorized absence and may lead to disciplinary action as per the
Disciplinary Policy, including possible treatment as voluntary resignation in extreme cases as
per the employment contract.

8. LEAVE DURING NOTICE PERIOD
Leave requests during an employee's notice period are subject to manager and HR approval
and are evaluated based on handover and business continuity requirements.

9. CARRY FORWARD AND LAPSE
- Casual Leave does not carry forward and lapses at the end of the leave year
- Sick Leave: up to 6 days may carry forward
- Privilege Leave: up to 30 days may be accumulated; balances beyond this lapse unless
  encashed as per the Employee Benefits policy

10. CONTACT
For questions about this policy or to report leave balance discrepancies, contact:
HR Helpdesk: hr.helpdesk@lumelight.com (demo address)
