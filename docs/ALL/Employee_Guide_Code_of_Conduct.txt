1. WELCOME TO LUMELIGHT
This guide introduces new and existing employees to Lumelight's culture, expectations, and
standards of conduct. It is intended to be a quick-reference companion to the detailed
department-specific policies (Leave, Disciplinary, POSH, Dress Code, Benefits, etc.).

2. OUR VALUES
- Integrity: We act honestly and transparently with clients, colleagues, and partners
- Client-first: We deliver consistent, high-quality service across BPO, LPO, and support functions
- Respect: We treat every individual with dignity, regardless of role, background, or seniority
- Continuous improvement: We seek to learn, document, and improve our processes constantly
- Accountability: We own our work and its outcomes

3. WORKING HOURS AND ATTENDANCE
- Standard working hours are 9 hours per day inclusive of a 1-hour break, 5-6 days a week
  depending on process requirements
- Shift-based teams (BPO/voice) follow rosters published by team leads at least 1 week in
  advance
- Employees must log attendance via the biometric system or HR portal at the start and end of
  each shift
- Habitual lateness is addressed under the Disciplinary Policy

4. CODE OF CONDUCT

4.1 Professional Behavior
- Treat colleagues, clients, and visitors with courtesy and respect at all times
- Avoid behavior that could be considered discriminatory, harassing, or intimidating
- Resolve workplace disagreements professionally; escalate to your manager or HR if needed

4.2 Confidentiality and Data Protection
- Client data, employee records, and internal business information must never be shared
  outside authorized channels
- Do not discuss client-specific information on personal social media or with unauthorized
  third parties
- Report any suspected data breach immediately to IT Security and your manager

4.3 Conflict of Interest
- Employees must disclose any personal or financial interest that could conflict with Lumelight's
  business interests (e.g., a close relative working for a competing vendor)
- Employees may not accept gifts or favors from clients/vendors beyond nominal value (as
  defined in the Gifts and Hospitality guideline) without disclosure to their manager

4.4 Use of Company Assets
- Laptops, phones, access cards, and software licenses are provided for business use; reasonable
  personal use is permitted unless it interferes with work or violates IT policy
- Employees are responsible for safeguarding company assets issued to them and must report
  loss or damage immediately to IT/Admin

4.5 Anti-Bribery and Anti-Corruption
- Lumelight strictly prohibits offering, giving, or receiving bribes in any form, whether to/from
  clients, vendors, or government officials
- Any suspected instance of bribery or corruption should be reported via the Whistleblower
  channel described below

4.6 Workplace Safety
- Employees must follow all safety instructions, fire drill procedures, and emergency protocols
  communicated by Admin/Facilities
- Report any unsafe condition or near-miss incident to your manager or the Facilities team
  immediately

5. WHISTLEBLOWER MECHANISM
Employees who witness or suspect any violation of this Code of Conduct, financial irregularity,
or unethical practice can report it confidentially via:
- Email: ethics.helpline@lumelight.com (demo address)
- Anonymous web form linked on the HR intranet
All reports are reviewed by an independent committee, and retaliation against whistleblowers is
strictly prohibited and treated as gross misconduct.

6. SOCIAL MEDIA GUIDELINES
- Employees may identify themselves as Lumelight employees on personal social media but
  should clarify that views expressed are personal and not those of the company
- Do not post confidential client or business information, even in general terms
- Be mindful that public posts can reflect on Lumelight's reputation

7. PERFORMANCE AND GROWTH
- Performance reviews are conducted twice a year (mid-year and annual)
- Goals are set in consultation with your reporting manager at the start of each review cycle
- Training and upskilling opportunities are communicated via the Learning & Development team

8. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
Employees with a workplace grievance (unrelated to POSH, which has its own dedicated
process) may raise it with their manager first, and escalate to HR if unresolved within 7 working
days. HR will acknowledge all grievances within 2 working days and aim to resolve them within
15 working days.

9. SEPARATION AND EXIT
- Standard notice period is 30-60 days depending on role/grade, as specified in the employment
  contract
- Exit interviews are conducted by HR to gather feedback
- Full and final settlement is processed within 45 days of the last working day, subject to
  clearance from IT, Finance, and Admin

10. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
All employees are required to read this guide and the related policies and provide
acknowledgment via the HR portal during onboarding and at each annual policy refresh.

11. CONTACT
For any questions, reach out to HR Helpdesk: hr.helpdesk@lumelight.com (demo address)
