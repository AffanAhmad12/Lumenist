

# Human Resources (HR) Policy

## 1. Purpose

The purpose of this HR Policy is to establish guidelines and expectations for employees and ensure a professional, safe, and productive work environment.

## 2. Employment

* All employees shall comply with company rules and regulations.
* Employment may be subject to a probation period as specified in the appointment letter.
* Employees are expected to perform their duties responsibly and ethically.

## 3. Working Hours

* Standard working hours are from **9:00 AM to 6:00 PM**, Monday to Friday.
* Employees must maintain punctuality and attendance.
* Overtime, if required, must be approved by management.

## 4. Attendance and Leave

* Employees must inform their manager in advance for planned leave.
* Sick leave should be communicated as soon as possible.
* Unauthorized absence may result in disciplinary action.

## 5. Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to:

* Treat colleagues, clients, and visitors with respect.
* Maintain professionalism at all times.
* Avoid harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior.
* Protect company property and confidential information.

## 6. Equal Opportunity

The company provides equal employment opportunities to all employees regardless of age, gender, religion, race, disability, or other protected characteristics.

## 7. Workplace Safety

* Employees must follow all safety procedures.
* Any accident, injury, or unsafe condition should be reported immediately.
* A safe and healthy work environment must be maintained by all employees.

## 8. Confidentiality

Employees must not disclose confidential company information, client data, business strategies, or proprietary information without authorization.

## 9. Use of Company Assets

* Company equipment, software, and resources should be used for business purposes.
* Employees are responsible for maintaining company property entrusted to them.

## 10. Performance Management

* Employee performance may be reviewed periodically.
* Feedback and development opportunities will be provided to support growth and improvement.

## 11. Disciplinary Action

Violation of company policies may result in:

* Verbal warning
* Written warning
* Suspension
* Termination of employment

depending on the severity of the violation.

## 12. Policy Amendments

The company reserves the right to modify or update this policy at any time. Employees will be informed of significant changes.
